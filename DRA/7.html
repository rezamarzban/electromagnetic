CPW feeding structures are widely used for millimeter-wave applications especially when the DRA is integrated in a system on chip (SoC). As a matter of fact, by using CPW transmission lines, it is possible to achieve high antenna eﬃciency since the ground plane separates the dielectric resonator from the lossy silicon substrate. 3.4.
